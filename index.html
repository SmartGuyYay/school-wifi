When you first try to connect to the school wi-fi, there are some important steps that you need to follow. This guide aims to help you get your internet up and going! If you follow the steps, and your internet is still not working, try looking in the FAQs.

Part 1 - Wifi Connection

If you've not connected to the school network previously, you need to follow some extra steps before you can access wi-fi.

-----------------------------------------

Step 1

Navigate to the app "Comp Portal" and tap "Sign In".

-----------------------------------------

Step 2

Type in your MIS email ("your MIS"@eq.edu.au).

-----------------------------------------

Step 3

Type in your MIS (without @eq.edu.au at the end) and your password (You will have been told your password by your teacher at the start of the year).

-----------------------------------------

Step 4

Then press the "begin" button, and follow the steps that the app will give you.

-----------------------------------------

Step 5

Open the settings app and click on "Profile Downloaded" which is located above the wifi setting.

-----------------------------------------

Step 6

Tap on "install".

-----------------------------------------

Step 7

Type in your passcode/password.

-----------------------------------------

Step 8

Tap on "install" again.

-----------------------------------------

Step 9

Tap on "install" once again.

-----------------------------------------

Step 10

Tap on "trust".

-----------------------------------------

Step 11

Go to Settings > General > VPN & Device Management and check that you have a profile named "Management Profile".

-----------------------------------------

Step 12

Go to the "Comp Portal" app, and tap on "Continue".

Part 2

If you've already connected to the school wifi, there are two main problems that can cause your internet to stop working. Here are some solutions to some common reasons that your wi-fi may not work.

Solution 1

-----------------------------------------

Tap on "Settings".

-----------------------------------------

Enter your MIS username and password.

-----------------------------------------

Done! Your internet should be working again.

Solution 2

-----------------------------------------

Tap on the "I" button.

-----------------------------------------

Tap on "Configure Proxy".

-----------------------------------------

Tap on "Automatic".

-----------------------------------------

Type in "http://autoproxy.eq.edu.au/".

-----------------------------------------

Tap on save.

-----------------------------------------

Done! Your internet should be working again.
